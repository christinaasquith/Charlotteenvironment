We are volunteer citizens of Charlotte who care about our community. We want to advise the selectboard, developers and the public on ways to avoid pollution, protect wildlife habitats and corridors, and adhere to our Town Plan

Raise Awareness

We speak out on Front Porch Forum, talk to town journalists, speak at public meetings and host events

We are a grassroots community, led by women, in which all neighbors are welcome.

  • A town gov with strong environmental representation and accountable governance
  • A town with strong, active civic engagement and participation especially by women and parents of school-age kids
  • A town with flourishing wildlife, protected natural environment and respect for habitat and open space

With creativity and community, we can grow and PREVENT:

  • Reckless, over-development
  • Sprawl in rural districts
  • Polluted natural environment
  • Stripped away regulations
  • Violated wildlife corridors
